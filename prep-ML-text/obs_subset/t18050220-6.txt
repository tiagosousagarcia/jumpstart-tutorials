What age are you?
Fifteen years and eight months.
What is your father?
A dealer, his name is Jacob Solomon : On the 4th of February , I was going to Mr. Neate, in Rossiter-street, St. John's-street-road, about seven o'clock, I was stopped in St. John's-street-road by two men, I believe the prisoner at the bar is one of the men, I cannot say I am certain of it.
Was any of your property found afterwards?
No.
Was any body present at all at the time?
No, nobody at all.
I apprehended the prisoner, the boy pointed him out; I found nothing on him.
