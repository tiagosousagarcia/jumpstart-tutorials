On Thursday, the 9th of August, the things were stolen; Mrs. Lloyd lodges with me; the prisoner was chairing for Mrs. Lloyd.
On the 7th of August, the prisoner pledged the things with me.
- I took the prisoner into custody, and saw her give a duplicate to a girl, which I took from her.(The things produced and identified.)
Mrs. Lloyd owed me one shilling and sixpence; and said, I might take something to pledge for it. GUILTY , aged 46.
